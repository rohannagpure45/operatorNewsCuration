## Breaking down Nvidia’s unusual $20 billion deal with Groq...

- Nvidia is paying $20 billion for a non-exclusive license to Groq’s inference technology rather than a full corporate acquisition.
- Groq founder Jonathan Ross and President Sunny Madra will join Nvidia, while Simon Edwards takes over as Groq's CEO.
- Groq will continue to operate as an independent company despite the significant financial and personnel arrangement with Nvidia.
- The deal focuses on Groq's LPU (Language Processing Unit) technology, which is optimized for fast and predictable AI inference compared to general-purpose GPUs.
